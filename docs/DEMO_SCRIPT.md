# 🎥 Vyapar Setu - Demo Video Script

Follow this step-by-step guide to record a perfect 2-minute demo video for your hackathon submission.

## 🛠️ Phase 1: Preparation (Do not record this)

1.  **Start the Application**:
    - Ensure Docker Desktop is running.
    - Open terminal in project folder: `cd c:\Users\Admin\Desktop\BharatBizAgents\bharat-biz-agent`
    - Run: `docker compose up -d`
    - Wait 30 seconds for it to start.

2.  **Open Windows**:
    - **Browser Tab 1**: Open `http://localhost:8000/dashboard`. (zoom to 100% or 110% for clarity).
    - **Telegram App**: Open your bot chat. Clear history if you want a clean start (`/start`).
    - **PDF Viewer**: Have a PDF viewer ready or just use browser preview.

3.  **Arrange Screen**:
    - Ideally, place Telegram on the left and Browser on the right (Split Screen) so judges see both updates happening. Or just switch tabs smoothly.

---

## 🎬 Phase 2: Recording (Start Recording Now)

### **Scene 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:15)**
*   **Visual**: Show the **Web Dashboard** (Overview Page).
*   **Voiceover**: "Hi, this is Vyapar Setu, an AI-powered business assistant for Indian shopkeepers. It bridges the gap between simple chat interfaces and powerful business management."

### **Scene 2: Creating an Order (0:15 - 0:45)**
*   **Visual**: Switch to **Telegram**.
*   **Action**:
    1.  Type `/start` (or just show the menu).
    2.  Click the button **"📝 Naya Bill (Create Order)"**.
    3.  Type: `Raju 500` (Simulating a quick verbal order).
    4.  The Bot will ask for confirmation. Click **"Yes"**.
    5.  The Bot will generate a PDF. **Click to open the PDF** briefly to show it's real.
*   **Voiceover**: "The shopkeeper simply types 'Raju 500'. The AI understands the customer name and amount, generates a professional invoice instantly."

### **Scene 3: Sending to Customer (0:45 - 1:00)**
*   **Visual**: Back to **Telegram**.
*   **Action**:
    1.  The Bot asks to send to customer? Click **"Yes"**.
    2.  Enter a phone number: `9876543210`.
    3.  Bot confirms: "Virtual Sent".
*   **Voiceover**: "The invoice can be sent directly to the customer's WhatsApp or Telegram, making the process seamless."

### **Scene 4: Real-time Sync & Inventory (1:00 - 1:30)**
*   **Visual**: Switch to **Web Dashboard**.
*   **Action**:
    1.  **Refresh the page** (if needed).
    2.  Point to the "Recent Orders" list showing "Raju - ₹500".
    3.  Click on the **"Inventory" tab**.
    4.  Show stock levels.
*   **Voiceover**: "Instantly, the order is synced to the central dashboard. Inventory is automatically deducted, giving the owner full control."

### **Scene 5: Stock Query via Bot (1:30 - 1:50)**
*   **Visual**: Switch back to **Telegram**.
*   **Action**:
    1.  Type (or use microphone if you can simulate): `Stock check karo`.
    2.  Scanning... Bot replies: "Total stock is [X] items."
*   **Voiceover**: "The owner can also check business stats just by asking in Hinglish, without opening the dashboard."

### **Scene 6: Conclusion (1:50 - 2:00)**
*   **Visual**: Show the GitHub Repo or Project Title Slide.
*   **Voiceover**: "Vyapar Setu empowers every local business to go digital with zero learning curve. Thank you."
